WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

The 4 quiet tricks I keep finding.

01

The line you never agreed to

€4-12 every month, every month, for a "Pack", "Protection" or "Smart Solar" service you don't remember signing up for. You'll know exactly where it hides on the bill — and how to cancel it in one call.

02

The "safe" tariff that costs you €270/year

Sold to you as "Plan Estable" or "fixed for peace of mind". Often the most expensive tariff you can be on. You'll learn to spot it in seconds — and what to switch to without losing the peace of mind.

03

Paying for a structure you never use

Your tariff is built for cheap night-time electricity. Nobody told you. You pay for the structure, you don't get the discount. You'll learn how to actually use it — or switch to a flatter tariff that suits you.

04

Paying for power you never needed

5,75 kW contracted. Your house uses 2,4 kW max. That's €60-80/year quietly wasted. You'll learn how to check your real usage in 2 minutes — and lower contracted power from your phone.
